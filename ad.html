Bronco Table Tennis Club

When Do We Practice?

We practice on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 11:30 AM to 1:00 PM and on Fridays from 4:00 PM to 6:30 PM

Where Do We Practice?

We practice on the 2nd floor of the BRIC in the MAC Courts.

Is there a skill requirement?

We are open to all levels of play. Beginners can receive instruction from experienced members or just play casually with one another. Those who wish to do drills, receive coaching, and compete against other schools can attend Friday night practices.
